Doctor-led

Compliance is the hard part. We do it properly.

UK rules restrict advertising prescription-only injectables to the public. Kay & Co. brings doctor-led judgement to aesthetics content, marketing your clinic and consultations without promoting prescription-only medicines by name, so you grow safely within ASA and MHRA guidance.

  • ASA and MHRA aware. No promotion of prescription-only injectables to the public.
  • Consultation-led. Market assessments and consultations, not specific named medicines.
  • E-E-A-T. Visible prescriber and practitioner credentials, insurance and review signals.
