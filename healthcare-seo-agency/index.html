Scope

What a healthcare SEO agency should cover.

The best work is not a pile of keywords. It is a joined-up system that makes services easier to crawl, understand, trust and choose.

  • Technical SEO. Indexation, crawl depth, speed, schema, redirects, canonicals and internal linking.
  • Service-page optimisation. Clear pages for treatments, clinics, locations, care services and provider categories.
  • Medical content strategy. Patient questions, referrer needs, local intent and safe explanations written in UK English.
  • Authority and AI visibility. Entity clarity, source links, structured data and concise answers for Google AI Overviews and answer engines.
