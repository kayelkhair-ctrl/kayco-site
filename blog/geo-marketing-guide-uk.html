GEO Marketing: A Guide for UK Businesses

By Kay & Co. / 10 June 2026 / 5 min read

Your customers are increasingly making decisions inside AI answers, asking ChatGPT which provider to choose, asking Perplexity to compare options, reading Google's AI Overview instead of clicking. GEO marketing is how your brand shows up in those moments. Here's what it is and how UK businesses can put it to work.

What is GEO marketing?

GEO marketing is marketing that uses Generative Engine Optimisation to make your brand appear and be recommended inside AI-generated answers. Instead of only competing for clicks on a results page, GEO marketing positions your brand where decisions are increasingly made: in the synthesised answer itself, across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews.

GEO marketing vs content marketing

They're complementary. Content marketing creates genuinely useful content for people. GEO marketing additionally structures that content so AI engines can understand, trust, and cite it, through clear entities, direct answers, and schema. In other words, GEO makes your content marketing discoverable in the AI era. It's the natural extension of AI SEO into your marketing strategy.

Content marketing earns attention from people. GEO marketing earns recommendations from the AI those people now ask.

Why GEO marketing matters for UK businesses

Because GEO is still new, the field is wide open. A focused UK business can become the brand AI recommends in its niche before larger, slower competitors react. And unlike paid channels, an AI citation keeps working, it's earned visibility that compounds, growing your AI visibility over time.

How to start with GEO marketing

  1. Audit your AI visibility. See how often AI engines mention you for the questions your customers ask.
  2. Define your entity. Make who you are, what you do, and where unmistakable, see LLM SEO.
  3. Create citation-worthy content. Factual, structured, question-led pages built around customer intent.
  4. Add structured data and ensure AI crawlers can read your site.
  5. Measure and refine. Track citation share and double down on what earns recommendations.

The takeaway

GEO marketing isn't a replacement for your existing marketing, it's the layer that makes it visible where buying decisions are increasingly made. For UK businesses willing to move early, it's one of the highest-leverage opportunities in marketing today. Kay & Co. helps you seize it.
