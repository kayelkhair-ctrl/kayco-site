Doctor SEO Keywords: 10 Semrush-Backed Page Ideas

By Kay & Co. / Updated 29 June 2026 / Semrush UK data

The mistake with doctor SEO is making one page chase every keyword. That creates duplicate content and weak intent. A cleaner plan is to split the cluster by what the searcher wants: service, agency, consulting, local SEO, website SEO, medical practice SEO and specialty pages.

This plan uses Semrush UK keyword data and an overlap audit of the existing Kay & Co. site. The existing SEO for doctors guide and SEO for medical practices guide stay informational. The new pages are commercial or specialty landing pages.

The 10 doctor SEO keywords and pages

How this avoids repeated pages

The cluster separates commercial, local, advisory, technical and specialty intent. That means doctor SEO services explains the service mix, local SEO for doctors owns map-pack visibility, and doctor SEO consulting is for advisory work.

The specialty pages, including SEO for surgeons and SEO for dermatologists, only exist where the searcher is clearly looking for a more specific doctor or clinic type.

What to write first

Start with the highest-demand and clearest commercial terms: SEO for doctors, doctor SEO services, local SEO for doctors and medical practice SEO services. Then use the specialist pages to support the cluster and capture long-tail demand.
