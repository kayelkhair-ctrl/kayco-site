How to Rank in Perplexity AI: A Perplexity SEO Guide

By Kay & Co. / 13 June 2026 / 5 min read

Of all the AI answer engines, Perplexity is the most transparent about its sources, it cites them right on every answer. That makes it one of the clearest opportunities in Generative Engine Optimisation: if you understand how Perplexity selects sources, you can earn those citations. Here's how Perplexity SEO works.

How Perplexity AI works

Unlike a model answering purely from memory, Perplexity is a retrieval-first answer engine. For each query it searches the live web, pulls the most relevant pages, synthesises an answer, and lists the sources it drew from with numbered citations. That means your path to visibility is concrete: be among the pages it retrieves and trusts for your target questions.

1. Win on relevance and freshness

Because Perplexity retrieves live content, relevance to the exact query and recency both matter. Create content that targets specific questions precisely, and keep your key pages updated. Stale, generic pages get passed over for sharper, fresher ones.

2. Structure for easy extraction

Perplexity lifts concise, well-organised passages. Use descriptive headings, lead with direct answers, and break information into short paragraphs and lists. The clearer your structure, the easier it is for the engine to quote you accurately. That same principle sits behind Answer Engine Optimisation.

Perplexity shows its sources. That transparency makes it the most measurable AI engine to optimise for.

3. Build authority and traditional rankings

Perplexity leans toward authoritative pages, and there's strong overlap with traditional search results. That makes solid SEO a direct asset: the authority and rankings you build for Google improve your odds of being retrieved by Perplexity too.

4. Let the crawler in and add schema

Make sure PerplexityBot is allowed in your robots.txt, your pages are fast and crawlable, and you've added structured data. These technical foundations decide whether your content can be retrieved and understood in the first place, part of broader LLM SEO.

The takeaway

Perplexity rewards exactly what good GEO produces: relevant, fresh, well-structured, authoritative content. Because it shows its sources, it's also the easiest engine on which to measure your progress and grow your AI visibility. Kay & Co. helps UK brands earn those citations and turn them into customers.
