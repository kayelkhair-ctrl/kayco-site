AI Visibility: How to Make Your Brand Visible to AI

By Kay & Co. / UK SEO & GEO Consultancy / Updated 14 June 2026

For years, brands measured success by search visibility, where they ranked and how much traffic they earned. A new measure now sits beside it: AI visibility, your brand's presence across the AI systems that increasingly answer your customers' questions. This guide explains what AI visibility is, how to measure it, and how UK businesses grow it.

What is AI visibility?

AI visibility is how often and how prominently your brand appears and is cited across AI-powered search and answer engines, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. It is the AI-era equivalent of search visibility: instead of asking "where do we rank?", it asks "when AI answers questions in our space, do we appear, and how favourably?"

Why AI visibility is the new metric

As people shift from browsing links to reading AI answers, traditional rankings tell only part of the story. You can rank well and still be absent from the AI answer your customer actually reads. AI visibility captures what now matters most: whether your brand is in the conversation the AI is having with your customer.

You can rank number one and still be invisible to the customer reading an AI answer. AI visibility closes that gap.

How to measure AI visibility

AI visibility is measurable, even if the engines don't hand you a dashboard. The key dimensions:

  • Citation share, how often your brand is mentioned or cited across the major engines for your priority queries.
  • Prominence, whether you're named first, linked, or merely implied.
  • Sentiment and accuracy, how the AI characterises your brand, and whether it gets the facts right.
  • Coverage, across how many engines and query types you appear.

How to improve your AI visibility

Growing AI visibility draws on every discipline in the AI-search toolkit:

  1. Define your entity consistently across your site, schema, and the wider web, see LLM SEO.
  2. Publish factual, structured content that answers real questions directly.
  3. Optimise each surface. Cover Google AI Overviews, answer engines, and AI chat.
  4. Earn authoritative mentions so the models trust and repeat you.
  5. Monitor and iterate, track citation share and refine continuously.
