Google AI Overviews: How to Appear in AI Search Results

By Kay & Co. / UK SEO & GEO Consultancy / Updated 14 June 2026

If you've searched Google recently, you've seen them: AI-written summaries sitting above the familiar list of links. These are Google AI Overviews, and they are reshaping how people find information. This guide explains what AI Overviews are, how Google chooses which sources to cite, and how UK businesses can earn a place in them.

What are Google AI Overviews?

Google AI Overviews are AI-generated summaries that appear at the top of some search results, answering the query directly and citing a handful of source websites. They are Google's generative layer over traditional search, synthesising an answer from multiple pages and linking to the sources it drew from. For users, it's a faster answer. For brands, it's a new and valuable position: being one of the cited sources.

Why AI Overviews change the game

AI Overviews push the traditional results further down the page and sometimes resolve the query entirely, reducing clicks. But they also create opportunity: being cited in an Overview puts your brand at the very top of the page, often above the organic results you'd normally compete for. The brands that adapt gain prime visibility; those that don't lose ground quietly.

Being cited in an AI Overview is the new position zero, above the links, inside the answer.

How Google chooses sources for AI Overviews

Google hasn't published a formula, but the observable pattern is consistent. AI Overviews favour content that is:

  • Relevant and direct, it answers the specific question clearly.
  • Trustworthy, from a source with topical authority and good E-E-A-T signals.
  • Well-structured, clear headings, concise passages, and lists that are easy to extract.
  • Often already ranking, strong organic performance correlates with citation, so traditional SEO still matters.

How to appear in Google AI Overviews

Optimising for AI Overviews is a core part of Generative Engine Optimisation. The practical steps:

  1. Answer the query directly in the opening lines of a relevant section.
  2. Build topical authority with comprehensive, interlinked content around your subject.
  3. Structure for extraction, descriptive H2s, short paragraphs, and lists.
  4. Add structured data, FAQ, Article, and Organization schema.
  5. Keep ranking, maintain strong traditional SEO, since it feeds the Overview.

AI Overviews and the wider AI search shift

Google AI Overviews are one front in a broader move toward AI-mediated discovery that also includes ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. Optimising for all of them together is the goal of AI SEO and AI visibility. Kay & Co. helps UK brands win across every surface at once.
