Digital PR: Building Authority That Ranks & Gets Cited

By Kay & Co. / UK SEO & GEO Consultancy / Updated 14 June 2026

Great content and clean technical SEO get you to the start line. Authority is what wins the race, and the most effective way to build it is digital PR. It's how brands earn the trusted references that move both Google rankings and AI citations.

What is digital PR?

Digital PR is the practice of earning coverage, links, and mentions from reputable online publications and sources to build a brand's authority and visibility. It blends traditional public relations, storytelling, relationships, newsworthiness, with SEO goals like earned links and brand mentions. Done well, it makes the wider web vouch for you.

Why authority matters so much

Search engines and AI models can't directly measure how good you are, so they rely on proxies. The strongest proxy is what credible third parties say about you. Earned links and mentions from trusted sites are among the most powerful ranking signals, and they're equally important for being recognised as a credible entity, the heart of E-E-A-T.

You can claim authority all you like. Digital PR is how the rest of the web confirms it, to Google and to AI.

How digital PR works

  • Newsworthy assets. Original research, data, and stories that journalists and sites want to cover.
  • Outreach & relationships. Connecting with relevant publications, writers, and creators.
  • Earned links & mentions. Coverage that links to, or names, your brand.
  • Reputation building. Consistent, credible presence across the sources that matter in your sector.

Digital PR and AI citations

Here's the modern twist: AI answer engines trust brands that are widely and credibly referenced across the web. Every quality mention digital PR earns strengthens your entity and makes models more likely to recognise and cite you. In other words, digital PR is now a core lever for GEO and AI visibility, not just classic SEO.

The takeaway

Authority isn't bought with spammy links, it's earned through genuine, newsworthy digital PR. That earned trust compounds across rankings, citations, and reputation. Kay & Co. builds authority for UK brands as part of a combined SEO and GEO strategy, explore our resources library.
