Zero-Click Search: What It Means for Your Brand

By Kay & Co. / UK SEO & GEO Consultancy / Updated 14 June 2026

For years, SEO success meant one thing: a click. But a fast-growing share of searches now end with no click at all, the user gets their answer and moves on. This is zero-click search, and it's quietly rewriting the rules of visibility.

What is zero-click search?

A zero-click search is one that ends without the user clicking through to any website, because the answer appears directly on the results page or is delivered by an AI. Featured snippets, knowledge panels, and especially Google AI Overviews all resolve queries in place. The information came from websites, but the visit never happened.

Why zero-click is rising

Search engines and AI assistants compete to answer questions as quickly as possible, and the fastest answer is one the user never has to click for. AI Overviews and chat assistants like ChatGPT and Perplexity accelerate the trend dramatically: they're built to deliver a finished answer, not a list of links.

In a zero-click world, the goal shifts from winning the click to winning the citation, being the source the answer is built from.

What zero-click means for your brand

It's tempting to see zero-click as pure loss, fewer visits. But there's opportunity: being the cited source in an answer builds visibility, authority, and brand recall even without a click, and often still earns the click for higher-intent queries. The brands that adapt capture attention at the exact moment of the answer; those clinging to click-only metrics quietly fade.

How to stay visible

  1. Optimise to be cited through GEO and Answer Engine Optimisation.
  2. Answer questions directly so engines lift, and attribute, your content.
  3. Strengthen your entity and brand so a mention means something even without a click.
  4. Focus clicks where they count, higher-intent, commercial queries that still drive visits.
  5. Measure citations, not just traffic, track your AI visibility.

The takeaway

Zero-click search isn't the end of SEO, it's a shift in what visibility means. The metric is moving from clicks to citations, and the brands that prepare now will own the answer. Kay & Co. helps UK brands stay visible as search changes, explore our resources library.
