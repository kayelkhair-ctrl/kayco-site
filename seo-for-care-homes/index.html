Why it matters

Families search carefully before choosing care.

Care home SEO is practical and local. Families compare location, care type, facilities, availability, reviews, inspection information, fees, visiting arrangements and the tone of the provider before they enquire.

Local SEO

Care decisions often begin with local searches such as care homes near me, residential care in a town or dementia care in a local area.

Service pages

Each care type needs a clear page that explains who it is for, what the home offers and how families can take the next step.

Trust signals

Photos, team information, reviews, inspection links, transparent contact details and helpful FAQs support confidence.

Content ideas

Helpful content supports both search and trust.

Care home SEO should answer the questions families ask before they are ready to call.

  • Location pages. Explain the areas served and connect them to nearby services, transport and practical family considerations.
  • Care-type pages. Residential care, nursing care, respite care and specialist care pages should each have a clear role.
  • Decision guides. Plain-English guides can answer common family questions without offering medical advice.
  • Technical basics. Fast pages, clean navigation, image optimisation, schema and consistent contact details help search engines and users.
