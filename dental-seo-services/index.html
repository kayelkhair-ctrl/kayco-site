Why specialist

Why dental practices need specialist SEO.

Dental search is unlike most local SEO. Patients compare practices quickly, treatments are competitive, and the content sits inside advertising rules most agencies do not work with every day.

Local and high-intent

Most dental searches are local and close to booking, so visibility near the patient wins the appointment.

Treatment-led pages

Implants, Invisalign, whitening, emergencies and routine care each deserve their own optimised page.

Trust and compliance

GDC registration, honest before-and-after use and careful claims keep you ranking and within ASA rules.

Scope

What our dental SEO services cover.

Not a pile of keywords, but a joined-up system that makes your treatments easier to find, trust and book, across Google and AI search.
