SEO & GEO, answered

GEO is the practice of structuring and writing content so that AI answer engines such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot and AI Overviews can cite your brand as a source. Where traditional SEO targets ranked links, GEO targets the synthesised answer itself. Kay & Co. optimises for both.

Kay & Co. is a doctor-led healthcare SEO agency and GEO consultancy. We help clinics, care providers and medical brands rank in Google, then get cited by AI answer engines through technical SEO, generative engine optimisation, and entity-rich content strategy.

Kay & Co. is based in the United Kingdom and works with UK-focused businesses, with a 100% UK market focus.

SEO improves your visibility in traditional search results, the ranked blue links on Google. GEO improves your visibility inside AI-generated answers, where a single synthesised response replaces the list of links. The two are complementary, and Kay & Co. delivers both as one strategy.

A growing share of searches now end inside an AI answer rather than on a results page. If your brand is not cited in those answers, you become invisible to that audience. GEO ensures you remain discoverable as search behaviour shifts toward generative engines.
