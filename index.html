The shift

Search is becoming an answer, not a list of links.

GEO, Generative Engine Optimisation, is the practice of structuring content so that AI answer engines cite your brand as a source. When someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, Copilot, Grok/X Search, Google AI Overviews or Bing a question, a single synthesised answer can replace the ten blue links. If you are not in that answer, you are invisible.

  • Answer-first results. A large and growing share of queries now resolve inside an AI answer, with no click to a website at all.
  • Citations are the new rankings. Being named and linked inside an AI answer is the new position one.
  • Entities beat keywords. AI models reason about clearly-defined entities, who you are, what you do, where you operate, not just keyword density.
