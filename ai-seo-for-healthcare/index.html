AI search visibility

Ranked, understood and citable.

AI SEO is not a platform partnership or a shortcut. It is the work of making your brand, services, locations and expertise clear enough for search engines and AI systems to understand.

Definition

What is AI SEO?

AI SEO is the practice of optimising a brand so it can be discovered, understood and cited across AI-powered search experiences, including Google AI Overviews and answer engines such as ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity. GEO, Generative Engine Optimisation, is the discipline focused on earning visibility inside generated answers.

AI Overviews

Google AI Overviews can summarise information before a user clicks. Healthcare brands need content that is clear, structured and source-worthy.

Answer engines

ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity can shape discovery by summarising options and citing sources. The goal is to be accurately understood and considered.

Traditional SEO

Strong crawlability, relevance and authority still matter. AI visibility is strongest when it builds on a healthy SEO foundation.

Healthcare GEO

Healthcare brands need visibility beyond ranked links.

Patients, families and decision-makers increasingly ask conversational questions. An AI answer may mention providers, summarise services or cite sources before the user visits a website.

  • Entity clarity. Make the organisation, services, locations and audience unambiguous across the site.
  • Topical authority. Build connected pages that show depth across healthcare SEO, medical SEO, provider SEO and care services.
  • Trust signals. Use clear authorship, transparent service information, structured data and consistent brand facts.
  • Extractable answers. Write concise sections that answer real questions without overclaiming or offering medical advice.
