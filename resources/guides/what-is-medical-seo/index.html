What Is Medical SEO?

Medical SEO helps medical websites become easier to find, understand and trust on Google, local search and AI search engines.

Meaning

What medical SEO means

Medical SEO is search engine optimisation for medical websites. It includes technical SEO, service pages, patient-intent content, local search and trust signals such as authorship, sources and review processes.

  • Make pages crawlable, indexable and fast.
  • Explain services clearly for users and search engines.
  • Support credibility through E-E-A-T and YMYL awareness.

Healthcare SEO

Medical SEO vs healthcare SEO

Medical SEO usually refers to clinical, treatment or medical practice websites. Healthcare SEO is broader and can include care providers, health technology, private clinics and medical brands.

  • Medical SEO often needs stronger clinical nuance.
  • Healthcare SEO can include care, wellness and provider services.
  • Both need careful claims and trustworthy content.

Local search

Local SEO for medical practices

Many medical searches are local. Patients often want a provider near them, opening hours, contact details, reviews and a clear explanation of available services.

  • Google Business Profile accuracy.
  • Location and service pages.
  • Internal links from guides to appointment or contact routes.
