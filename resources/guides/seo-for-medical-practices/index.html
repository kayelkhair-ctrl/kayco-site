Services

Make every important service findable

A medical practice needs more than one generic services page if people search for specific treatments or clinics.

  • Create pages for meaningful service lines.
  • Use plain headings and clear next steps.
  • Link related FAQs and guides back to service pages.

Local

Strengthen local discovery

Patients often search by service plus location, so practice pages need local signals and consistent business data.

  • Keep Google Business Profile details accurate.
  • Create useful location/service relationships.
  • Track calls, forms and booked consultations.

Trust

Protect accuracy and confidence

Medical practice content should be useful without drifting into individual medical advice.

  • Use doctor-led input for clinical pages.
  • Keep claims specific and supportable.
  • Add schema and internal links for clarity.
