Foundation

Start with crawlable, indexable pages

Medical websites often lose visibility because important pages are hard to find, duplicated or technically unclear.

  • Check indexability, canonicals and status codes.
  • Keep the sitemap focused on useful public pages.
  • Make mobile templates fast and easy to read.

Service pages

Build service pages around real intent

Patients and referrers need specific pages for services, locations, eligibility, costs and next steps.

  • Give each important service a clear page.
  • Explain who the service is for without overpromising.
  • Use internal links to connect related services and resources.

Trust

Make expertise visible

Healthcare content needs stronger evidence, review standards and author signals than ordinary commercial content.

  • Show who is responsible for content.
  • Use careful language around symptoms and outcomes.
  • Add Article, FAQ and breadcrumb schema where useful.
