Technical

Indexing and technical SEO checklist

Start by making sure search engines can access the pages that matter and understand their purpose.

  • Important pages are indexable and listed in the sitemap.
  • Canonicals, redirects and status codes are clean.
  • Core pages are fast enough and mobile friendly.

Local

Google Business Profile and local SEO checklist

Clinics and local providers need consistent business data, service areas, location pages and review signals.

  • Name, address, phone and opening hours are accurate.
  • Services and categories are clear.
  • Location pages link to relevant services and contact routes.

Content

Service page and medical content checklist

Each service page should explain who the service is for, what the provider offers, where it is available and how to take the next step.

  • Pages match patient or client search intent.
  • Claims are careful and not exaggerated.
  • Content shows E-E-A-T and YMYL trust signals.

Structure

Schema, internal linking and reporting checklist

Structured data, internal links and conversion tracking help search engines understand the site and help teams measure whether visibility is turning into enquiries.

  • Breadcrumb, FAQ, Article or Service schema where relevant.
  • Related pages link to each other naturally.
  • Forms, calls and key enquiries are tracked.
