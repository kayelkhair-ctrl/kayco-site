Company role

What a healthcare SEO company does

A healthcare SEO company builds the search system around your services: the technical base, the service pages, the resources, the local signals and the content trust needed for high-intent visibility.

  • Organic search strategy for clinics and care providers.
  • Service pages for patient and client acquisition.
  • Local SEO, internal linking, schema and reporting.

Marketing and SEO

Healthcare SEO vs healthcare marketing

Healthcare marketing can include paid ads, social media, brochures and campaigns. Healthcare SEO focuses on long-term organic visibility where people are already searching for services or answers.

  • Search demand mapped to services and locations.
  • Content built around patient and referrer intent.
  • GEO work for AI search citations and answer-ready content.

Choosing well

How to choose the right healthcare SEO company

Look for a company that understands medical content risk, tracks enquiries rather than only rankings, and can explain how technical SEO, E-E-A-T, YMYL and AI search fit together.

  • Ask who writes and reviews medical content.
  • Check whether local SEO and schema are included.
  • Avoid guaranteed rankings or vague traffic promises.
