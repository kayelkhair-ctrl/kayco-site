What an agency does

What a healthcare SEO agency does

A healthcare SEO agency improves how medical and care websites are found, understood and trusted in search. The work covers technical foundations, service-page structure, local visibility, useful resources, internal links, schema and content quality.

  • Map patient, family, referrer and buyer search intent.
  • Build service pages around clear healthcare demand.
  • Improve crawlability, internal links, structured data and content depth.

Why it is different

Healthcare SEO is not normal SEO with medical words added

Medical websites sit in a higher-trust environment. Pages may shape how people compare care, understand treatments or decide whether to make contact, so the content needs accuracy, careful claims and visible credibility.

  • Clear E-E-A-T signals for expertise and trust.
  • YMYL caution around claims and sensitive topics.
  • Local SEO for clinics, care providers and service areas.

Doctor-led content

Medical judgement comes before the marketing layer

Kay & Co. starts with doctor-led content thinking, then applies SEO and GEO. That means the page is planned around medical accuracy, user intent and search structure from the first draft.

  • Doctor-led content planning and writing.
  • Source-aware, answer-ready paragraphs for AI search.
  • Service pages that stay useful without making medical advice claims.
