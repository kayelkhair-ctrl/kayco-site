Consulting scope

What a healthcare SEO consultant does

A consultant reviews your current site, identifies blockers and creates a focused plan for technical SEO, content, local search, schema and AI visibility.

When to hire

When expert advice is the right first step

Consulting suits teams that already have developers, writers or marketers but need healthcare-specific direction. It also helps before a rebuild, migration or major content expansion.

  • Before changing website structure or URLs.

Clinic visibility

Local SEO, technical SEO and schema

Many clinics lose visibility because Google cannot clearly connect services, locations, practitioner signals and business profile data. A focused audit finds those gaps and turns them into a plan.

  • Google Business Profile and local landing page checks.
  • Schema for services, organisation details and FAQs.
  • Internal links between related pages and conversion paths.
