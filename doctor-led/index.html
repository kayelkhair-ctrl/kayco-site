Written, not just reviewed

Most healthcare content is checked late. Ours starts right.

There is a real difference between content a doctor reviews and content a doctor writes. One bolts accuracy on at the end. The other builds it in from the first sentence.

The usual model

Medically “reviewed”

  • A generalist writer drafts the page from secondary sources.
  • A clinician is asked to skim and sign off at the end.
  • Errors and vague claims are patched, not prevented.
  • The “reviewed by” badge does the trust-signalling.
The Kay & Co. model

Doctor-written

  • A doctor writes the page, so clinical judgement shapes every line.
  • Accuracy, nuance and safe boundaries are there from the first draft.
  • Plain language without losing clinical precision.
  • The trust is in the content itself, which is what Google and AI read.

Why it matters

In healthcare, accuracy is the ranking strategy.

Healthcare is high-trust, “Your Money or Your Life” content. Search engines and AI answer engines are built to be cautious with it, so the most reliable path to visibility is being genuinely accurate and authoritative.

E-E-A-T by default

Experience, expertise, authoritativeness and trust are not bolt-ons when a doctor writes the page. The signals Google looks for are present because the expertise is real.

Citation-ready for AI

AI answer engines cite sources they can treat as correct. Accurate, clearly-bounded, doctor-written content is the kind ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and AI Overviews can quote with confidence.

Patient trust, protected

Careful language earns the confidence of patients, families and referrers, and avoids the unsafe or unsupported claims that put a healthcare brand at risk.

How it works

Clinical judgement first. Search engineering around it.

Doctor-led does not mean less SEO. It means the search work is built on content that is right in the first place, then made easy for Google and AI engines to find, read and cite.

  • Doctors write the substance. The clinical content, the claims and the boundaries are set by people who understand the medicine.
  • Accuracy is designed in. Nothing is “fixed in review” because it was never wrong to begin with.
  • Search discipline carries it. Technical SEO, entities, schema and internal links turn trusted content into visibility across Google and AI search.
