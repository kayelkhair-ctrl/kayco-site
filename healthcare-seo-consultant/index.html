When to use it

Useful when the question is not just who can write more pages.

Healthcare SEO consultancy is strongest when you need judgement. That could mean diagnosing why rankings have stalled, deciding whether a medical topic is safe to publish, or turning a scattered content plan into a search strategy.

  • Before a rebuild. Protect indexation, service pages, redirects and existing visibility before design work begins.

  • After performance drops. Review technical issues, content quality, cannibalisation and competitor pressure.
  • For AI search readiness. Clarify entities, answers, schema and source signals for AI visibility.
